The Mexican Minister of Defense

From Guadalajara to Grand Prix Glory

Sergio "Checo" Perez is Red Bull Racing's reliable and experienced driver, known for his exceptional race management and tire preservation skills. The Mexican driver has proven himself as an invaluable teammate to Max Verstappen, contributing significantly to Red Bull's championship success.

Starting his F1 career with Sauber in 2011, Perez has showcased his talent across multiple teams including McLaren, Force India, and Racing Point before joining Red Bull in 2021. His ability to deliver consistent results and his tactical race approach make him one of the most reliable drivers on the grid.

Known as the "Mexican Minister of Defense" for his defensive driving prowess, Perez continues to play a crucial role in Red Bull's championship campaigns. His experience and race craft make him an essential component of the team's success.
